Warning

Known Bug

A known Armorcore / Krom bug causes build crashes for Armory3D projects whenever they contain high resolution textures.

Note:

  • Textures with a image resolution of +4k or higher seem to trigger the crashes.
  • A special Armorcore / Krom build for Windows is available for download.
  • Additional details on how to fix the bug for macOS and Linux builds are also provided.
